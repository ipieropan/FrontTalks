Responsive design

Responsive design is an approach to web development that ensures websites function optimally across a wide range of devices and screen sizes. With the increasing variety of devices used to access the internet—such as smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops—responsive design has become essential for delivering a consistent and user-friendly experience.
