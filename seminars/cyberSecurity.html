Cyber Security

Cybersecurity refers to the collection of practices, technologies, and processes designed to protect computers, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, or damage. In an increasingly digital world, the importance of cybersecurity cannot be overstated. It safeguards sensitive information, helps maintain the integrity of data, and ensures the availability of services, thereby playing a crucial role in both personal and organizational security.
